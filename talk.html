MUD CONSTRUCTION.

What's there in it

Architecture involves use of various different construction materials that require a unique construction technique. But most of these construction techniques are energy intensive techniques. All the natural resources are depleting which has made it mandatory that we choose materials and construction systems which require less energy for its execution. Mud construction system is less energy intensive, easily available, cheap, and strong ,very effective in different climatic conditions and required only simple technology . While all previous attempts to fails replace with a more plastic, environmental friendly material building with mud offers unique possibility of a synthesis. As an exploration into the possibilities of mud as building material is especially relevant in country like India. The advantages and possibilities of mud construction are endless. Mud construction system is less energy intensive and very effective in different climatic conditions.

What's GIS

GIS provides an easy and more efficient way to interpret geospacial data to create a topographic map with elevation , angles and other features. This workshop expose the participants to the concepts and applications of GIS in use for multiple disciplines.
