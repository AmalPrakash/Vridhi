What's there in it

CADbury comes up with all the skills and ideas that you can show up with CADD. This event will evaluate your talents in conquering the ruling AUTOCAD 2D.

1.This an event with 2 members and both of them needs to draw the plan, and elevation based on the given plan within the given time constraints.
